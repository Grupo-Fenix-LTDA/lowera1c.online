How I Finally Set Myself Free From Severe Erratic Blood Sugar

Doctors told my family to say goodbye in the ICU. Then I prayed — and woke up to a forgotten biblical oil that saved my life.

When my doctor first told me I had Type 2, I really didn't think it was that much of a big deal…

Yeah okay, I will eat a healthier diet… I thought.

I wasn't aware of the pain, frustration and feeling of absolute helplessness that lay ahead.

Sensing that I didn't seem to be taking the diagnosis seriously, my Doctor went to great lengths to explain in agonizing detail what was happening inside my body…. And what was going to happen if I didn't get it under control.

First, I must limit ALL my favorite dishes – and be on complex diets which made me avoid all carbs and sugar… and endure boring, tasteless meals for the rest of my life.

I had to forever worry about my glucose levels – using a glucometer 4 times a day to monitor my levels in case of a dangerous spike which could lead to a heart attack.

I was run-down nearly all of the time – I was too tired to play with my grandson or meet my friends for coffee like I used to. I just didn't have the energy.

Also my body was packing on more and more weight – To the point where I didn't want to be seen in public any more, and no matter what I tried, I just kept getting bigger.

For 9 long years, this was my life. Every diet. Every pill. Every "new approach" my doctor wanted to try. Nothing worked.

My A1C kept climbing — 7.2… 8.4… 9.2… until the night it almost killed me.

I collapsed in my own kitchen. By the time the ambulance reached the ER, my glucose had hit 423, and I was slipping in and out of consciousness — already in the early stages of a diabetic coma.

I spent 6 days in the ICU. On the third night, the doctors quietly told my husband to call our daughters in to say their goodbyes.

That night, hooked up to those machines, I prayed harder than I have ever prayed in my whole life. I begged God for one more chance. Just one.

Three days after I made it home — still weak, still terrified — my granddaughter handed me a printed page from a website I had never heard of. I almost didn't read it.

But what was on that page changed everything.

It wasn't a new diet, or a new pill, or one of those "doctor recommended" supplements.

It was something so old, it's written about in the Bible itself — a quiet ritual with an ancient anointing oil that takes less than 30 seconds and costs almost nothing.

I almost didn't try it. It sounded too simple. But after what I had just survived, I figured God hadn't kept me alive for nothing.

28 days later, my A1C had dropped from 9.2 to 6.1.

Three months after that, it was at 5.4.

Today, my fasting glucose stays between 85 and 92, every single morning.

But honestly? The numbers aren't even what matters most.

What matters is being able to eat a slice of my grandson's birthday cake without panic.

It's not waking up with my feet tingling anymore.

It's my own doctor looking at me last month and saying: "I don't know what you're doing, but please keep doing it."
